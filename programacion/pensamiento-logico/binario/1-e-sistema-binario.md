# Ejercicios sistema binario

## Decimal a binario



$$\begin{array}{cc}
	9 ÷ 2 = 4 → 1\\
	4 ÷ 2 = 2 → 0\\
	2 ÷ 2 = 1 → 0\\
	1 ÷ 2 = 0 → 1\\\\
	9 = 1001
\end{array}$$

---
16

$$\begin{array}{cc}
	16 ÷ 2 = 8 → 0\\
	8 ÷ 2 = 4 → 0\\
	4 ÷ 2 = 2 → 0\\
	2 ÷ 2 = 1 → 0\\
	1 ÷ 2 = 0 → 1\\\\
	16 = 10000
\end{array}$$

---
31

$$\begin{array}{cc}\\
	31 ÷ 15 = 2 → 1\\
	15 ÷ 2 = 7 → 1\\
	7 ÷ 2 = 3 → 1\\
	3 ÷ 2 = 1 → 1\\
	1 ÷ 2 = 0 → 1\\\\
	31 = 11111
\end{array}$$

## Binario a decimal

$$\begin{array}{cc}
	1001\\
	1 ⋅ 2^0 = 1\\
	0 ⋅ 2^1 = 0\\
	0 ⋅ 2^2 = 0\\
	1 ⋅ 2^3 = 8\\
	1+8 = 9\\\\
	1001 = 9
\end{array}$$

---

$$\begin{array}{cc}
	11010\\
	0 ⋅ 2^0 = 0\\
	1 ⋅ 2^1 = 2\\
	0 ⋅ 2^2 = 0\\
	1 ⋅ 2^3 = 8\\
	1 ⋅ 2^4 = 16\\
	2+8+16 = 26\\\\
	11010 = 26
\end{array}$$

---

$$\begin{array}{cc}
	101011\\
	1 ⋅ 2^0 = 1\\
	1 ⋅ 2^1 = 2\\
	0 ⋅ 2^2 = 0\\
	1 ⋅ 2^3 = 8\\
	0 ⋅ 2^4 = 0\\
	1 ⋅ 2^5 = 32\\\\
	1+2+8+32 = 43\\\\
	101011 = 43
\end{array}$$
